Our Story

As Barb remembers, 'It was all a dream, I used to read Beer Brew magazine. Barley, hops, and malt up in the limousine.' Those were the days before she closed the doors on her old life and opened the doors of her brewery. On that opening day, August 7th, 1995, a star was born. Yes, we went there. Her first brew, The Barb, was a meaty trip for the senses. And with that, Barb gave the world the greatest gift of all: The gift of great beer. From there, minds and waistlines grew, history was made, and she has never looked back.
